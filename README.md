# Operational Intelligence Platform

## Vision

Every business generates enormous amounts of operational data every day.

Shipments are scanned.
Packages are sorted.
Drivers perform deliveries.
Customers create orders.
Warehouses process inventory.

Most of this data is stored, but very little of it is transformed into actionable knowledge.

The goal of the Operational Intelligence Platform is to bridge the gap between raw operational data and business decision making.

Instead of asking:

> What data do we have?

We ask:

> What truths are hidden inside the data?

---

## Mission

Build a unified intelligence layer on top of operational systems to:

* Expose business performance in real time
* Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies
* Discover operational trends and anomalies
* Support strategic decision making
* Provide AI-assisted business insights

The platform should transform operational events into business intelligence.

---

## Guiding Principles

### Data First

Reliable intelligence starts with reliable data.

Before introducing AI, dashboards, or analytics, operational events must be captured, modeled, and structured correctly.

### Business Driven

Technology is not the goal.

Every metric, report, dashboard, and AI insight should answer a real business question.

### Single Source of Truth

Operational intelligence should be generated from a consistent and trusted data model.

### Explainable Intelligence

Insights should be traceable back to the underlying operational events and business processes.

# Development Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Operational Data Layer

Objective:

Transform operational systems into structured analytical datasets.

Potential components:

* Event Tables
* Fact Tables
* Aggregated Metrics
* Daily Snapshots
* Data Quality Monitoring

Examples:

* Shipment Facts
* Package Facts
* Delivery Attempt Facts
* Driver Performance Facts
* Warehouse Throughput Facts

---

## Phase 2 - Business Intelligence Layer

Objective:

Expose operational performance through dashboards and reporting.

Potential tools:

* Metabase
* Power BI
* Grafana

Key Areas:

### Executive Dashboard

* Revenue
* Shipment Volume
* Service Level
* Failed Delivery Rate

### Warehouse Dashboard

* Throughput
* Processing Time
* Productivity
* Bottlenecks

### Delivery Dashboard

* Delivery Success Rate
* Attempt Analysis
* Driver Performance

### Customer Dashboard

* Customer Volume
* Profitability
* Service Quality

---

## Phase 3 - Intelligence & AI Layer

Objective:

Enable natural language access to business knowledge.

Examples:

### Daily Operational Summary

Automatically generate executive summaries from operational data.

### Ask the Business

Examples:

* Why did failed deliveries increase yesterday?
* Which depot is underperforming?
* Which customer generates the most operational cost?

### Predictive Intelligence

Examples:

* Delay prediction
* Demand forecasting
* Failure risk detection
* Resource planning
